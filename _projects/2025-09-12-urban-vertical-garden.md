## Overview
**Project Type:** Sustainable Engineering Design<br>
**Duration:** June 2025 - October 2025<br>
**Team:** 5 Members<br>
**My Role:** Lead Decision Analyst

A sustainable urban planning project proposing a scalable rooftop micro-garden system optimized for high-rise residential environments.

## Problem Statement
Lack of space in high-density urban housing restricts access to fresh produce and disconnects residents from sustainable food sources.

## Objectives
* Propose a cost-effective, space-efficient garden layout.
* Use decision matrices to validate design alternatives.
* Develop a localized bill of materials for deployment.

## Engineering Process
Literature review, development of multi-criteria weighted decision matrix, system design in CAD, and structural weight analysis.

## Technical Details
10m² footprint; Budget: 74,200 LKR; Utilized lightweight organic substrates.

## Design Decisions
Selected Green Rooftop architecture for its high social and environmental impact score.

## Tools & Software
* AutoCad
* Spreadsheet Analysis
* Decision Matrices

## Engineering Skills Demonstrated
* Systems Engineering
* Decision Analysis
* Resource Allocation

## Challenges
Accounting for structural weight limits on existing rooftops.

## Results
Delivered a fully validated, CAD-modeled design proposal within strict budget constraints.

## Key Learning
Learned to convert qualitative sustainability goals into quantitative decision-making parameters.
